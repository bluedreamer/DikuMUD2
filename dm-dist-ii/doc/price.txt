This document lists basic prices for items in Valhalla. Please use
for  guidelines  as well as the price multipliers  listed  in  the
Object Creator Documentation.

1.1

Basic currency
1 Platinum piece (pp)    = 8 Gold pieces
1 Gold piece (gp)        = 8 Silver pieces
1 Silver piece (sp)      = 8 Copper pieces
1 Copper piece (cp)      = 8 Iron pieces (ip)
1 Iron piece (ip)        = 10 old gold pieces

In order to survive the day (on bread and water) you would need  a
little less than 1 cp daily.

A  wealthy farmer would have a monthly profit of approximately  30
cp every month (3 sp and 6 cp).
